If you want to install and use MAGIX Photo Manager Delux 17, your system must meet the following minimum requirements:

* **OS:** Windows 10
* **RAM:** 512 MB
* **Processor:** 1.83 GHz
* **Graphics card:** Onboard with minimum 1024 x 600px resolution
